Why we keep things simple.

Async-friendly

Take your turn at lunch, when the kids are in bed, or from a coffee shop across the country — bots fill seats between moves.

Private rooms

Friends-only tables with passwords, or open rooms anyone on the lobby can join. Chat, pegs, and meld history included.

Fair + friendly

Bots are rule-based, not machine-learning. They play well but never cheat, and they never show up on leaderboards.
